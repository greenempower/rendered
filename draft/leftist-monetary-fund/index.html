GEM Draft: A Leftist Monetary Fund

A leftist monetary fund can act as a shared international fund across for all leftist entities.

Essentially the fund can act as a large centralized bank complex.

The fund will naturally by owned and operated by the entire leftist community.

The fund will ask that member entities pay into it when they are in good position to do so so that other entities can take from the pool when they are in need or weakend.

The fund itself will do work to manage and grow its funds.
