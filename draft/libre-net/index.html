GEM Draft: Libre Network

We call for a network of general purpose computing devices that is completely free of interference and regulation of the fascist complex (government and it's corporations).

These devices will be manufactured locally and largely use long range mesh communications for networking.

The libre network will also need to become independ of neoliberal authorities who are subject to enforcement by parts of the fascist complex such as the legal system. (ex. a domain belonging to an independent actor cannot be forfeited on the whim of a fascist.)
