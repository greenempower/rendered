Prescriptions for the United States of America

There shall be a democratization of the economy.

Industries/companies that are subject to nationalization will be nationalize. Specifically those which act as utilities or serve as substantial infrastructure will be subject.

The government will refrain from generously granting our tax dollars to private enterprise.

There will be a well funded and well connected intellectual state of millions of researchers, scientists and intellectuals.

There will be a large scale expansion of the deliberative infrastructure. Millions of Americans will participate meaningfully in the politics of the nation.

Social/economic classes and class structures will be phased out.

There will be a push against propriety in most if not all of our systems.

Patent and intellectual property will be eliminated in favor of common ownership of knowledge and ideas.

Reparations and reinvestements will be made marginalized communities and care will be taken to address inequities along racial, ethnic and historical lines.

The military and police will be wholesale defunded. That money will be reinvested in our communities and our programs that will insure continued innovation and prosperity.
