Applied Behavioral Analysis

ABA is a 'therapy' meanly used in 'treating' autism.

In practice it tends to be highly abusive and degrading to the recipient.

I was diagnosed with ASD when I was 15. Shortly afterwards I was enrolled in an ABA clinit in my town.

The place was alright superficially.

The owner of the place was very rude to me and tried to put me down.

It became clear that this therapy was about degradation, submission and training.

It was sorta dehumanizing so I created a scene and accosted some therapists until they couldn't handle me anymore.
