Here is a flag for representing your support of communism while claiming your rightful stake in America and your identity as an American communist/socialist.

The flag is version 1.0. It is essentially a minimum viable product and you can expect further improvement by me and hopefully others (including you :)).

Please feel free to adapt it.

The hammer and sickle portion of this design was taken from the logo of Communist Party USA. I found the design here.
