Identity Accounting System of The Green Empower Organization; Green Empower ID (GEMID)

Green Empower will administer an ID for individuals and entities and it will be titled GEMID.

GEMID will simply act as an accounting system accross our services with the aim of creation a unique ID for each identity of a person or other organizational body.

I shall aim that we mostly don't need to use this system in the operation of our services as I see it as possibly infringing upon the freedom and anonymity of peoples.
