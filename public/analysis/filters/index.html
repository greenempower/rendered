Natural and Political Filters

There seem to be a number of filters in place in society that among other things determine what type of people come to power and what they do when they are in power.

Filters are highly relevant in the context of capitalism as they seem to manifest themselves very negatively here.

We wonder why all of these people in power seem to be so greedy, but maybe that is a filter. Maybe we are allowing the greediest of all people assume power consistently.
