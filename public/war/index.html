GEM: War

Globally we are in a constant state of war.

Within the US we have the class war which is being waged even though many Americans are unaware of it.

Globally we have the new cold war. Major powers are militarizing and competing in the power space.

In the new cold war the two major players are essentially the USA and China. Most everyone else is probably aligned with one or the other.

Of course then there are greater internation organizations such as the European Union and the United Nations. This is an area of further learning for me.

As far as I'm concerned the new cold war is bogus. Both countries are further militarizing (China plans to reach parody with the US military in ~20 years.) and we can't have that.

This war will do nothing to improve our lives and I will assume that the USA is the primary instigator here as usual.

Globally we should seek demilitarization and cooperation. The more so we can achieve the better off we as the people of this earth will be.
