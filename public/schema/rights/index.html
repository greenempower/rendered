Green Empower Declaration of Rights

This is an ever expanding set of rights that should be afforded to all people.

We do expect for these to be built and expanded upon with time as development and technology facilitate.

Fundamental Rights

The right to clean water for drinking and enough of it to live healthily without deprivation.

The right to healthy food and enough of it to live healthily without deprivation.

The right to space and shelter necessary to live life healthily without deprivation.

The right to association with other people necessary for healthy mental development and sanity.

Basic Rights

The right to knowledge and basic primary education so that he is given to capacity to learn by his own direction.

The right to self determination alongside his piers so that they have power over to decisions made that affect their condition.
