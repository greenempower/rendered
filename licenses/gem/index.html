The GEM License

The GEM is a leftist license that adheres to the philosophy of The Green Empower Organization.

This license works to facilitate the transition from Capitalism to Socialism and then from Socialism to a higher order society in which we are all prosperous, engaged and self-determinant.

This license will adapt based on the government of which it is being subject to. This is necessary as not all societies are equal in their political development.

It is expected that this license is treated with respect by all who interact with it.
